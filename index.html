🧠 What Your Game Reveals About AI

Your Training Phase mirrors how AI models learn — the more patterns you identified, the better your "model" performed in testing.
When you picked only one pattern to rely on, predictions got worse — that's overfitting, a real AI problem.
Confidence scores you gave each prediction reflect how AI assigns probability values — not just yes/no, but how sure the model is.
Cards you couldn't predict highlight noise in the data — random patterns AI systems also struggle with.
Your score represents your model's accuracy — real AI benchmarks work the same way: predicted vs. actual outcomes.
